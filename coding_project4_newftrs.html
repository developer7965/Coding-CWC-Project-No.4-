New Qualification Rules in Future World Cups


For the World Cup, the top seven sides plus the hosts (India in this case) from the thirteen competitors in the Super League will qualify. The remaining five teams, along with five Associate sides, will play in the Cricket World Cup Qualifier, from which two teams will go through to the final tournament. The main route for qualification will be the ICC Cricket World Cup Super League tournament.
